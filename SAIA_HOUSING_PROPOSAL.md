## THE CHALLENGE: UK SUPPORTED HOUSING IN 2025

The supported housing sector is under unprecedented pressure:

### Regulatory Burden
- **Supported Housing (Regulatory Oversight) Act 2025** introduces new licensing requirements
- **Tenant Satisfaction Measures (TSM)** reporting now mandatory under Social Housing (Regulation) Act 2023
- Building safety compliance and Awaab's Law (October 2025) requirements
- Increased scrutiny from Regulator of Social Housing with financial penalties for non-compliance

### Operational Pressures
- **1 in 3 providers** closed schemes in 2024 due to funding constraints
- Support staff spend **15+ hours per week on administrative tasks** that could be automated
- Manual documentation processes create compliance risks and inefficiencies
- Paper-based support notes and incident records difficult to audit and analyze

### Digital Transformation Gap
- Many providers still rely on spreadsheets and paper records
- Existing housing management systems not designed for supported housing workflows
- Support workers need mobile-first tools to document during resident visits
- Lack of integration between property management and resident support systems

**AccommodateME solves these challenges with purpose-built technology.**

## THE SOLUTION: ACCOMMODATEME PLATFORM

AccommodateME is a comprehensive multi-tenant SaaS platform that manages the complete resident lifecycle from onboarding to ongoing support, compliance tracking, and incident management.

### Core Platform Features

#### 1. Resident Management & Onboarding
- **Digital onboarding workflows** replacing paper forms
- **Document verification** with encrypted secure storage (AES-256-GCM)
- **Tenancy allocation** with automatic room availability validation
- **Comprehensive resident profiles** with support history and risk assessments

**Business Value:** Reduces onboarding time from days to hours, eliminates data entry errors, ensures GDPR compliance from day one.

#### 2. Weekly Support Session Notes
- **Mobile-optimized forms** for documenting support sessions on-site
- **Every Child Matters criteria** (5 wellbeing categories built-in):
  - Economic Wellbeing
  - Enjoy & Achieve
  - Be Healthy
  - Stay Safe
  - Make a Positive Contribution
- **Attendance tracking** (Present, Authorized Non-Attendance, Did Not Attend)
- **PDF export** for audits and sharing with external agencies
- **Full audit trail** of all interactions

**Business Value:** Support workers complete documentation during visits, not back at the office. Eliminates 5-10 hours/week of admin per support worker.

#### 3. Incident Tracking & Reporting
- **Real-time incident logging** from mobile devices
- **Risk categorization** and escalation workflows
- **Regulatory reporting** templates for safeguarding and serious incidents
- **Trend analysis** to identify patterns and prevent future incidents

**Business Value:** Ensures no incidents go undocumented, provides evidence of duty of care, enables proactive risk management.

#### 4. Property & Room Management
- **Multi-property portfolio management** with hierarchical organization
- **Room allocation validation** preventing double-booking
- **Capacity tracking** for single and multi-occupancy rooms
- **Tenancy lifecycle** from move-in to move-out

**Business Value:** Eliminates allocation errors, maximizes occupancy, provides real-time availability reporting.

#### 5. Compliance & Document Management
- **Centralized document repository** with encryption at rest
- **Document verification workflows** (Pending → Verified → Rejected)
- **Compliance tracking** with automated reminders for renewals
- **Audit trails** for all document access and modifications

**Business Value:** Pass RSH inspections with complete audit evidence, reduce compliance officer workload, eliminate lost documents.

#### 6. Role-Based Dashboards
- **Platform Admin:** Cross-organization KPIs and system management
- **Organization Admin:** Full organizational oversight and reporting
- **Operations Manager:** Portfolio analytics and performance tracking
- **Support Worker:** Mobile-first task cockpit with assigned resident caseload

**Business Value:** Everyone sees exactly what they need - no more, no less. Mobile-optimized for field workers.

### Technical Architecture

#### Multi-Tenant Security
- **Complete data isolation** via organizationId foreign keys on all records
- **Row-level security** ensures organizations never see each other's data
- **JWT authentication** with HTTP-only cookies
- **Rate limiting** on all authentication endpoints
- **Helmet.js security headers** protecting against common web vulnerabilities

#### Data Protection & GDPR Compliance
- **AES-256-GCM encryption** for all uploaded documents
- **SHA-256 file hashing** for integrity verification
- **Time-limited download tokens** (15-minute expiry)
- **Complete audit logs** of all user actions and data access
- **GDPR-ready** data export and deletion capabilities

#### Reliability & Performance
- **99.5% uptime SLA** with 24/7 infrastructure monitoring
- **Neon PostgreSQL** serverless database (automatically scales)
- **Cloud-hosted** infrastructure with automatic backups
- **Mobile-responsive** design for tablets and smartphones
- **Progressive Web App** capabilities for offline access

#### Integration Ready
- **RESTful API** for third-party integrations
- **Webhook support** for real-time event notifications
- **Data export** in CSV, Excel, and JSON formats
- **Future compatibility** with MRI Software and Aareon QL housing management systems
